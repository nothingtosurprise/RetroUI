Bored with the same old "clean" layouts? I’m too. That’s why I’ve gathered 12 examples of neo-brutalist web design that throw standard UX out the window. 
Neobrutalist web design helps designers and developers build websites that look different and stand out.

**List of Awesome Neobrutalist Sites:**

## [1. RetroUI](https://www.retroui.dev/)
RetroUI, a neo-brutalist styled UI library that unapologetically embraces bold design. So you will immediately notice bold typography, vibrant and high-contrast colors, and thick, dark shadows everywhere.
![RetroUI cover](/images/blogs/12-neobrutalist-websites-you-should-know-in-2026/retroui.jpg)

## [2. Gumroad](https://gumroad.com/)
Gumroad lets creators sell their products online. I’m impressed by its bright colors and illustrations, highlighting that a marketplace can look good without the usual “slick” design.
![Gumroad cover](/images/blogs/12-neobrutalist-websites-you-should-know-in-2026/gumroad.jpg)

## [3. Feastables](https://feastables.com/)
MrBeast’s snack brand uses neobrutalism to connect with a younger, Gen-Z audience. The site features bold, chunky buttons and sticker-like graphics, creating a high-energy, tactile feel.
![Feastables cover](/images/blogs/12-neobrutalist-websites-you-should-know-in-2026/feastable.jpg)

## [4. Kristi Digital](https://kristi.digital/)
Next, Kristi Digital is a simple and straightforward portfolio website. The navigation is easy, and everything feels neatly organized.
![Kristi Digital cover](/images/blogs/12-neobrutalist-websites-you-should-know-in-2026/kristi%20digital.jpg)

## [5. GT Maru](https://gt-maru.com/)
This site is a great example of how to use animation well. The neobrutalist design makes the animations clear and meaningful.
![GT Maru cover](/images/blogs/12-neobrutalist-websites-you-should-know-in-2026/GT%20maru.jpg)

## [6. Rewind](https://rewind.youtube/)
Rewind lets users explore the past through a mosaic grid that feels like a real collection of memories.
![Rewind cover](/images/blogs/12-neobrutalist-websites-you-should-know-in-2026/rewind.jpg)



## [7. Roze Bunker](https://rozebunker.nl/)
This Dutch drink brand uses bright neon colors and a bold layout to showcase a DIY-inspired neobrutalist style.
![Roze Bunker cover](/images/blogs/12-neobrutalist-websites-you-should-know-in-2026/Roze%20Bunker.jpg)

## [8. DesignThinkers](https://www.designthinkers.com/)
Next up is the official website for RGD’s annual design conference. It uses bold typography, clear sectioning, and high-contrast layouts to present speakers, schedules, and event details in an organized and accessible way.
![DesignThinkers cover](/images/blogs/12-neobrutalist-websites-you-should-know-in-2026/design-thinkers.jpg)

## [9. FPL Radar](https://fplradar.com/)
FPL Radar, a data-heavy site for Fantasy Premier League fans. It uses neobrutalism to make complex statistics more readable by boxing them into high-contrast cards that pop against the background.
![FPL Radar cover](/images/blogs/12-neobrutalist-websites-you-should-know-in-2026/FPL%20Radar.jpg)

## [10. Curry Cafe](https://curry.cafe/)
Next up is Curry Cafe, an Indian food website designed in a neobrutalist style. It breaks traditional design rules and may feel challenging to navigate at first, but the bold design makes it memorable.
![Curry Cafe cover](/images/blogs/12-neobrutalist-websites-you-should-know-in-2026/Curry%20Cafe.jpg)

## [11. Lydiaamarush portfolio website](https://lydiaamaruch.com/)
There’s the portfolio website of Lydia Amaruch, a UI/UX and brand designer from Germany. The site is highly engaging, featuring interactive, animation-heavy elements that make browsing enjoyable.
![Lydia Amaruch cover](/images/blogs/12-neobrutalist-websites-you-should-know-in-2026/lydiaamaruch.jpeg)

## [12. Studio Job](https://www.studio-job.com/)
The site presents Studio Job’s work like a digital exhibition, using bold visuals, and raw typography in a neobrutalist style.
![Studio Job cover](/images/blogs/12-neobrutalist-websites-you-should-know-in-2026/studiojob.jpg)


## Conclusion:
And that wraps up a showcase of some of the best neobrutalist websites out there.
If you’re tired of regular templates, neobrutalism offers a bold and playful alternative. These sites are fun, unique, and full of ideas that can inspire you to think outside the box in your own designs.
